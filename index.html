From Bacteria to Beef

The human body is source of many types of bacteria, different parts of the body harbour different species. The species within an individual may vary from another. The hypothesis is that the ideal bacteria species to make synthetic beef may be found in the belly button. Data has been colected from multiple individuals about the bateria species that live on their belly buttons in order to share it alongside the participants and research team. They will be able to identify the bacteria species that live on their navels through the selection of their ID.
